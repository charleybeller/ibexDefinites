In this task we would like you to judge whether it is possible for two expressions to refer to the same person.

The expressions that to be considered will be overlined.

We are interested in your personal judgment on this matter so there are no wrong or right answers.

  • If you think that the indicated expressions can refer to the same person, then you should give the sentence a high rating (6 or 7).
  • If you think that they cannot then you should give the sentence a low rating (1 or 2).
  • Sometimes it may not be totally acceptable or totally unacceptable for the indicated expressions to refer to the same person, you could give those a more intermediate rating (3-5).
