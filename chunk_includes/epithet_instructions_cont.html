Sometimes sentences that look superficially similar have very different different coreference possibilites. The two sentences below have almost the same words; but according to the judgments of many speakers the bolded expressions in (1.) must refer to the same person, while the bolded expressions in (2.) cannot.

  1. Mary saw herself in the mirror.
  2. Mary saw her in the mirror.
Some of the sentences you will be asked to rate will look similar to one another but none of them are exactly the same.
